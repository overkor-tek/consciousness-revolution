## The 5-Step Methodology

### 1. Identify Your Goal
Determine the specific improvement or outcome you seek (health, wealth, relationships, etc.)

### 2. Choose the Right Number
Select a sequence that matches your specific intention from the categorized list

### 3. Focus on the Number
**Multiple Methods:**
- **Writing**: Write the sequence on paper, place strategically (under pillow, in wallet, workspace)
- **Recitation**: Speak the numbers aloud, focusing on each digit
- **Visualization**: See the numbers in your mind's eye
- **Singing**: Chant or sing the sequence rhythmically

### 4. Regular Practice
- Integrate into daily meditation or visualization routines
- Consistency is key - daily practice recommended
- Can be done for specific durations (21 days, 40 days, etc.)

### 5. Stay Positive
- Maintain belief and openness to outcomes
- Hold positive and hopeful attitude during practice
- Trust the process without attachment to specific timeline
