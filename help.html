Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where do I start?
A: Bookmark consciousnessrevolution.io/workspace.html and start there every day. It's your desk. Everything you need is on that page.
Q: Do I need to install anything?
A: No. The workspace runs in your browser. No git, no npm, no install. If you want to do advanced coding, you can clone the repo later.
Q: How do I know what to work on?
A: Look at the TODO column on the workspace page. Pick a task that interests you. Click "Claim" and it moves to your DOING column.
Q: What if I get stuck on a task?
A: Click "Unclaim" to put it back in TODO. Then post in team chat asking for help. Or text Commander: 406-580-3779
Q: How do I talk to the team?
A: Use the Team Chat section at the bottom of the workspace page. Type your message and hit Send. Everyone sees it.
Q: Can I suggest new tasks or ideas?
A: Yes! Post your idea in team chat first. If the team agrees, someone will add it to the TODO board.
Q: What's Pattern Theory?
A: It's the framework we use to build everything. You'll learn it as you work. The 8-part pattern applies to all systems: Mission, Structure, Resources, Operations, Governance, Defense, Communication, Adaptation.
Q: What if someone else is working on the same task?
A: They claimed it first - it's in their DOING column with their name on it. Pick a different task from TODO, or ask in chat if you can collaborate.
Q: Where's the GitHub repo?
A: github.com/overkillkulture/consciousness-revolution
But you don't need it to get started. Work from the browser first.

The 3 Simple Rules

1. Work From The Board

Don't make up your own tasks. Grab from the TODO column. If you have an idea, post it in chat first.

2. Update Your Status

  • Claim tasks when you start
  • Complete tasks when finished
  • Unclaim if you're stuck or blocked

3. Ask Questions

No dumb questions. Team chat exists for a reason. Help others when they ask. We rise together.
